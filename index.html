Fruit Flavoured E-Liquids 

Our fruit flavours are fresh and vibrant, offering options like apple, watermelon, mango, and mixed berries. These fruity blends deliver a burst of natural taste with every inhale, perfect for vapers seeking a refreshing, flavorful experience. 

Dessert Flavoured E-Liquids 

For those with a sweet tooth, our dessert-inspired E-liquids capture the rich, indulgent flavours of creamy vanilla custard, smooth chocolate, and caramelised sugar. These flavours are ideal for all-day vaping, bringing the comfort of your favourite sweets without the calories. 

Tobacco Flavoured E-Liquids 

Designed to replicate the classic taste of traditional smoking, our tobacco-flavoured E-liquids offer robust, familiar blends. These flavours are especially popular among former smokers looking for a smooth transition into vaping, with options that range from mild to bold. 

Menthol and Mint E-Liquids 

For a cool, invigorating experience, our menthol and mint flavours provide a refreshing burst with each puff. These flavours are perfect for vapers who enjoy the crisp, clean finish of icy mint or menthol, leaving a refreshing aftertaste. 

Salt Nicotine E-Liquids 

Salt nicotine offers a smoother throat hit with higher nicotine concentrations, making it a favourite among those transitioning from smoking. Our salt nic E-liquids provide a more satisfying nicotine hit without the harshness of traditional freebase nicotine, offering flavours that deliver both strong satisfaction and rich taste. 

Nicotine-Free E-Liquids 

For vapers who prefer the flavour without the nicotine, we carry a wide range of nicotine-free e-liquids. These options allow you to enjoy all the great flavours and cloud production without the nicotine content, perfect for those who have already weaned off or simply prefer nicotine-free vaping.

Frequently Asked Questions 

What are the most popular e-liquid flavours at Majestic Vapes? 

Our top-selling flavours include Apple Drop, Lemon Drop, and Berry Drop. Each offers a unique and rich vaping experience that keeps customers coming back.

What’s the difference between salt nicotine and regular nicotine?

Salt nicotine provides a smoother throat hit at higher nicotine levels, making it ideal for those who prefer a stronger nicotine hit without the harshness. Regular nicotine (freebase) delivers a more intense throat hit but is better suited for lower nicotine levels.

Are your e-liquids lab-tested for safety?
